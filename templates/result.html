The Depression Screening Test based on PHQ9 is the foundation for this depression test.

PHQ-9 is a self-administered depression screening tool used to determine the severity of depression in individuals. It consists of nine questions about common depression symptoms such as sadness or hopelessness, difficulty sleeping, and changes in appetite or energy levels. Each question's score is added up to provide a total score, with higher scores indicating greater depression severity. PHQ-9 is commonly used in clinical settings to aid in the diagnosis of depression and to assess treatment effectiveness.

You need to know something important.

If you are experiencing depression symptoms. These symptoms are most likely causing problems in your daily life right now. These issues may be severe, making it difficult for you to carry out daily tasks and enjoy your usual activities. You should seek assistance right away.

Get support.

When you're depressed, it can be difficult to summon the energy to make a phone call. However, having the support of others can be beneficial. Speak with your doctor or a therapist. They can assist you in obtaining depression treatment. You could also try telling your family and friends how you feel. You will not always feel the way you do now. People improve. There are numerous effective treatments available for depression.

About Us

"MindEase: Your Depression Management Companion" is a digital tool designed to assist individuals in managing their depression symptoms. The tool functions by administering a multiple-choice questionnaire to the user to determine their mental state. The goal of MindEase is to empower people suffering from depression to take control of their mental health and improve their overall well-being.
