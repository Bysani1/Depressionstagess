You need to know something important.

Depression is becoming more common nowadays, with an increasing number of people being diagnosed with the condition. High levels of stress, isolation and loneliness, social media pressure, and the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic are all factors. The current state of the world has made it more difficult for people to obtain necessary mental health care, resulting in an increase in untreated depression. Untreated depression can have serious consequences, lowering a person's quality of life and increasing the risk of suicide. As a result, it is critical for individuals to seek assistance and for society to prioritise mental health care.

About Us

"MindEase: Your Depression Management Companion" is a digital tool designed to assist individuals in managing their depression symptoms. The tool functions by administering a multiple-choice questionnaire to the user to determine their mental state. The goal of MindEase is to empower people suffering from depression to take control of their mental health and improve their overall well-being.
